PRODUCTION COMPANIES

Mapping: Production Companies Founded by Year

This time map shows the locations that African-American based production companies d by year. Visualizing these locations allows us to understand the nationwide spread of independently founded production companies. This time map also gives us a more clear picture of the trends of early African-American race film production. If you’d like to make your own map with this data, you can see our tutorial.

Mapping: Geographical Expansion of Film Companies

This still map shows the geographical expansion of the film companies that contributed to the distribution and production of these Early African American films. We can visually see the strong influence and presence of the East in the development and creation of such films. This allows for future researchers to dive into the historical and social reasoning behind this.
